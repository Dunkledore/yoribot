About Yori

Yori started as a project to bring as many features as possible to a public bot. Over time it became clear that Yori could do a lot of things but not as well as we would like, and like every other bot on Discord he was a bit tricky to learn. In the summer of 2018 our primary developer, Dunkledore began the process of rewriting Yori completely with a different outlook on what he would become. Some of the main things we had in mind for this rewrite were:

  • User Friendly Help Command
  • Admin and Moderation Commands
  • Auto-moderation and Spam Prevention
  • Robust, Clean, and Useful Logs
